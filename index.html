Now for the formal introduction…Ever since a young age I’ve been incredibly sensitive to the energies around me. I saw and heard things most could not and I always had an interest in the paranormal at an incredibly young age. I started becoming curious about the occult at around 18 years old. I purchased my first tarot deck on whim at Barnes & Noble. For years, it would remain untouched until I had my spiritual awakening at 23 years old. I reached a point where I needed clarity in my own life.
